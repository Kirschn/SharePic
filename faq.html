FAQ:


  • What is PicDrop?


    A service to share images in the cloud.

  • Why and when should I use PicDrop?


    Why? Because it's super easy and fast! Use it whenever you want to share pictures or the service you use has no feature to embed pictues. Just drop the link into your conversation.

  • Which image formats can I upload to PicDrop?


    Wayyyy too many. Have a quick look at this table.
    PROTIP: .GIF, .JPEG and .PNG Files aren't auto-converted, so you can upload them even faster

  • Am I allowed to integrate PicDrop into my application? If yes, how?


    For sure! Just POST your HTML Form File Object to http://picdrop.com/upload.php in the field "file".
    It will return the Image ID in the answer body.
    The Image will be available under these URLS:
    • http://picdrop.tk/myID1 (HTML Preview Page)
    • http://picdrop.tk/myID1/raw (Will return a 302 Header to the location of the Image in our CDN)

  • Can I auto-upload Images from ShareX to PicDrop?


    Yup! But it's dangerous to go alone, take this http://picdrop.tk/KnH58 and this http://picdrop.tk/sharex.json
